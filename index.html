Shoreline Hotspot Finder

Draw an area on the map, click RUN, and download hotspot points (CSV / KML).

If the app doesn’t load, Open in new tab

How to use

  1. Draw a box or polygon covering your area of interest.
  2. Set Cloud %, Threshold, and Shoreline distance.
  3. Click RUN to see hotspot dots.
  4. Click Generate downloads to save CSV or KML of hotspot point locations.

Tips

  • Large areas may take longer; try raising threshold or drawing smaller areas.
  • CSV uses lat, lon coords — ready for Google Maps paste.
  • Spots are candidates — field-check for debris vs algae or foam.
